# 002 / reflection — motion script

Master timeline: 360 frames @ 30fps. The 4:5 and 9:16 versions share the
timeline; the label auto-raises on the tall (Reel) frame.

| t | beat | description |
|---|---|---|
| 0.0–1.0s | paper breathes | Empty cream field. Dither grain drifts almost imperceptibly. No subject. |
| 1.0–2.5s | the sun | The dot materializes on the horizon — no drop, no arrival. It fades into existence in place, as if it was always just out of view. It's the source. |
| 1.5–4.0s | horizon | The line writes on left→right via a mask wipe. Slow start, confident middle, tiny settle. Hand wobble and the left fork preserved. The dot's columns are punched from the line sprite, so the center stays open until the dot is there. |
| 2.3–7.8s | the reflection (focus) | The reflection leaks from the sun. It begins seeping downward even before the dot has fully solidified — the water can't wait. The sprite's own ink gaps make it read as dwindling dabs dripping from the source, the sun's light leaking on its past. A damped vertical shimmer adds watery life and reaches 0 by the beat end, so the still reads crisp. |
| 7.8–10.0s | hold + breath | One breath: scale 1.000 → 1.012 → 1.000. Imperceptible camera push-in runs the whole shot. Grain keeps drifting. |
| 10.0–11.5s | the label | A small dithered gallery label fades in bottom-left (title + message). End frame doubles as the still. |

The sun is the source. The reflection is the part that moves. The water
doesn't copy the dot — it answers, breaking the light into pieces the deeper
it falls. The reflection leaks on the sun's past.

Secret (every post): the glyph R hidden in the dither field, integrated into the
dots themselves — coarser dot size within the letter's mask, zero color
difference. Accumulates across posts into GROWTH (002 = "R").

Sound direction (chosen at post time): dry and organic with a watery accent —
a soft drip per reflection dab, low room tone under it.
